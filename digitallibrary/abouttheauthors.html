Brandon Sanderson

Brandon Sanderson is a highly acclaimed American author known for his contributions to the fantasy and science fiction genres. He is recognized for his imaginative world-building, intricate magic systems, and compelling characters. Sanderson's notable works include the "Mistborn" series, the "Stormlight Archive" series, and his completion of Robert Jordan's "The Wheel of Time" series. He is also known for his meticulous and prolific writing, engaging with his readers through social media, and his contributions to the genre as both an author and educator, as he teaches creative writing at Brigham Young University. Sanderson has gained a devoted following of readers who appreciate his unique storytelling style and his dedication to expanding the boundaries of speculative fiction

Some of their works:

Mistborn Trilogy: The Final Empire, The Well of Ascension, The Hero of Ages

Stormlight Archive Series: The Way of Kings, Words of Radiance, Oathbringer, Rhythm of War

The Reckoners Series: Steelheart, Firefight, Calamity

Alcatraz vs. The Evil Librarians Series: Alcatraz vs. the Evil Librarians, The Scrivener's Bones, The Knights of Crystallia, The Shattered Lens, The Dark Talent

Legion Series: Legion, Legion: Skin Deep, Legion: Lies of the Beholder

Skyward Series: Skyward, Starsight

Stand Alone: The Rithmatist, Elantris, Warbreaker, White Sand

The Wheel of Time: The Gathering Storm, Towers of Midnight, A Memory of Light

J.R.R Tolkien

J.R.R. Tolkien, whose full name is John Ronald Reuel Tolkien, was an English writer, poet, philologist, and professor. He is best known for his contributions to the fantasy genre and is considered one of the most influential fantasy authors of the 20th century. Tolkien's works are celebrated for their rich world-building, intricate languages, and epic storytelling.

Some of their works:

The Lord of the Rings trilogy: The Fellowship of the Ring, The Two Towers, The Return of the King

The Silmarillion Legendarium: The Silmarillion, The Children of Húrin, The Unfinished Tales of Númenor and Middle-earth

The History of Middle-earth series: The Book of Lost Tales, Part I, The Book of Lost Tales, Part II, The Lays of Beleriand, The Shaping of Middle-earth, The Lost Road and Other Writings, The Return of the Shadow, The Treason of Isengard, The War of the Ring, Sauron Defeated, Morgoth's Ring, The War of the Jewels, The Peoples of Middle-earth

Other Books in Middle Earth: The Hobbit
